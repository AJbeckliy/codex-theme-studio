# Rights modes

This checklist reduces accidental misuse; it is not legal advice.

## original

Use original characters, symbols, patterns, colors, and compositions. Avoid official tournament names, logos, crests, exact trophies, sponsor marks, and identifiable public figures unless separately cleared.

## licensed

Use only when the user has permission covering the exact artwork, marks, distribution, and intended commercial use. Record the license scope in `rights.notes`; do not embed private license files in a public theme package.

## personal-ip

Use for the user's own brands, mascots, products, and commissioned assets. Confirm that third-party fonts, stock art, and generated source material permit the planned sharing.

## private-reference

Use for a non-public personal prototype based on user-supplied third-party reference images or likenesses. State that the theme must not be redistributed or published. This mode records the limitation; it does not grant rights.

## Preflight

- Are all visible logos and marks original or licensed?
- Is the character or person original, fictional, or cleared?
- Is any trophy, jersey badge, product shape, or mascot too close to an official asset?
- Are generated image outputs free of accidental text, watermarks, or recognizable marks?
- Does `rights.notes` state the actual source and intended use?
